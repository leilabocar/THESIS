Grave Finder is use to locate the tomb of the deceased person at the Map of the Himlayang Cemetery.

Follow the instructions below.

Step 1: Click Grave Finder at the Homepage.

Step 2: Click the Search Box.

Step 3: Type the name of the your Loved ones that buried at Himlayan.

Step 4: Click Search.

Step 5: At the table, look for the name of that you search.

Step 6: Click the View Destination.

Step 7: Look at the Map at the right side and you can see now the location of the tomb of your loved ones.

Step 8: You can now follow the directions that the map presented when you go the Himlayang Cemetery.

Forms are used to send a request about buying the lots, renting a lot, and booking an appointment at the Himlayang Cemetery. When you click forms, there are three choices: the Application Form for renting a lot, the Buyers Form for buying a lot, and Book Appointment for booking a schedule at Himlayang Office.

How to Fill-Up the Forms?

Step 1: Click Application Form, if you want to rent a apartment lot.

Step 2: Click Buyers Form, if you want to buy a memorial lot type.

Step 3: Click Book Appointment, if you want to schedule an appointment meeting at the Himlayan Office.

Step 4: After choosing at the three choices and pick one, fill up the form correctly.

Step 5: After filling out the forms correctly, click submit or confirm to send your fill out form.

Step 5: Wait to the response message of Himlayang Office through email.

Inquiry Forms are used to send inquiries about a lot, whether available or not.

How to Fill-Up the Inquiry Form.

Step 1: Click Inquire at the Homepage.

Step 2: Click button of lots and choose what you lot you want know if it is available.

Step 3: Click Search.

Step 4: At the table, that's the available lots.

Step 5: Click View to know where is the directions of that available lots.

Step 6: Look at the Map below and you can see now the location of the that avaible lot at the Himlayang Cemetery.

Step 7: After knowing the location of the available lots, fill out the inquiry form correctly.

Step 8: After filling out the forms correctly, click confirm to send your fill out form.

Step 9: Wait to the response message of Himlayang Office through email.
